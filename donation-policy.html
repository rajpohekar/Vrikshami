Donation Policy

Placeholder Donation Policy: This policy is for demonstration purposes for Razorpay Go-Live application only. A complete, detailed Donation Policy will be implemented for the live website.

Thank you for supporting Vrikshami and our partner NGOs through your generous donations. Please review our Donation Policy below.

1. Donations are Voluntary

All donations made to Vrikshami through our Website are voluntary contributions towards the environmental conservation efforts of our listed partner Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs). You are donating to support their important work in reforestation and related projects.

2. Refunds (Generally Not Applicable for Donations)

As donations are charitable contributions supporting NGO projects, generally, donations are non-refundable. Please ensure you intend to donate before completing your transaction.

In Exceptional Circumstances: In very exceptional cases, such as a clear processing error or accidental duplicate donation, please contact us at contact@vrikshami.org as soon as possible, and we will review your request on a case-by-case basis. Any refunds granted will be at our discretion.

3. Donation Receipts and Acknowledgements

You will receive a confirmation email acknowledging your donation after your transaction is processed. This confirmation email serves as your receipt for donation purposes.

You may receive further updates or acknowledgements from the partner NGO you have chosen to support directly, depending on their individual practices, but Vrikshami primarily issues the initial donation receipt.

4. Contact Us Regarding Donations

If you have any questions or concerns about your donation, please contact us at contact@vrikshami.org.

Thank you again for your support!
