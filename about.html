Vrikshami is a social initiative committed to promoting environmental conservation and sustainability through tree adoption. We partner with reputable Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) in India who are dedicated to reforestation, biodiversity protection, and community-based environmental programs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make environmental action accessible and impactful for everyone. We provide a platform that connects individuals and businesses with trusted NGOs, making it easy to support vital reforestation projects and contribute to a greener planet.

What We Do

Vrikshami acts as a bridge between donors and NGOs. We:

  • Carefully vet and partner with impactful environmental NGOs.
  • Showcase NGO projects and their impact on our website.
  • Facilitate a simple and transparent tree adoption and donation process.
  • Raise awareness about the importance of reforestation and conservation.

Why Choose Vrikshami?

  • Trusted Partners: We work only with verified and reputable NGOs.
  • Transparent Impact: Your donations directly support on-the-ground conservation.
  • Easy Adoption: Simple online platform to make a difference.
  • Environmental Focus: Dedicated to creating a greener and healthier future.

Join us in our mission to Plant a Seed, Grow a Future!
