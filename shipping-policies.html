Shipping Policy

Shipping Policy for Donations - Not Applicable:

Vrikshami is a platform for tree adoption and environmental donations. We are not an e-commerce store selling physical goods, and therefore do not offer shipping for any physical products.

When you make a donation through Vrikshami, you are supporting the tree planting and conservation efforts of our partner Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs).

What you receive upon donation:

  • Digital Certificate of Adoption: After a successful donation, you will receive a digital certificate of appreciation, usually via email or downloadable from our website (as per specific NGO or platform features). This certificate is delivered electronically, not physically shipped.
  • No Physical Products: Please note again that no physical products or items are shipped to you as part of the tree adoption donation process. Your donation is directly used to support environmental projects.

This Shipping Policy is provided for informational purposes and to comply with website policy requirements. It clarifies that no shipping of physical goods is applicable to the services provided by Vrikshami.
